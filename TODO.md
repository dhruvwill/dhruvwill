- headings
  Here are some professional-sounding headings for your portfolio that capture the essence of "experience" but with a more polished tone:

General Headings:

Professional Experience (Classic and straightforward)
Project History (Highlights the accomplishment aspect)
Skillset in Action (Focuses on skills demonstrated)
Career Highlights (Showcases key achievements)
Headings with a Twist:

The Journey Thus Far (Conveys a sense of continuous learning)
Building Expertise (Emphasizes your ongoing development)
Portfolio of Accomplishments (Combines experience with results)
Shaping My Path (Shows a proactive approach)
For "Recent Adventure" Section:

Beyond the Workday (Separates professional and personal experiences)
Fueling Creativity (Highlights how the experience benefits your work)
Exploring New Horizons (Focuses on growth and exploration)
A World Outside the Office (Creates a clear distinction)
Bonus Tip:

Consider using a subheading within the main section to further clarify the type of experience. For instance, under "Professional Experience," you could have subheadings like "Web Development Projects" or "Marketing Campaigns."
The best choice for you will depend on the overall tone and content of your portfolio.
